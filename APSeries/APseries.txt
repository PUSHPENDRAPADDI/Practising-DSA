Problem Statement: Given an A.P. Series, we need to find the sum of the Series.

a = first term of A.P.

d= common Difference of A.P.

n= Number of Terms in  A.P.

Examples:


 
Example 1:
Input:
n=4
a=2
d=2
Output: 20
Explanation: 2+4+6+8 = 20

Input:
n=8
a=2
d=5
Output: 124
Explanation: -2 +3 + 8 + 13 + 18 + 23 + 28 + 33 = 124
