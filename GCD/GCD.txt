Problem Statement: Find gcd of two numbers.

Examples:

Example 1:
Input: num1 = 4, num2 = 8
Output: 4
Explanation: Since 4 is the greatest number which divides both num1 and num2.

Example 2:
Input: num1 = 3, num2 = 6
Output: 3
Explanation: Since 4 is the greatest number which divides both num1 and num2.
