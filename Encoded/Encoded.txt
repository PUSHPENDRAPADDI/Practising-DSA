A company wishes to encodes its data. The data is in the form of a number. They wish to encode the data with respect to a specific digit. They wish to count the number of times the 
specific digit reoccurs in the given data so that they can encode the data accordingly. Write an algorithm to find the count of the specific digit in the given data.

Input
The input consists of two space-separated integers- data and digit, representing the data to be encoded and the digit to be counted in the data.

Output
Print an integer representing the count of the specific digit.

Input: 
572378233 3

Output:
3
