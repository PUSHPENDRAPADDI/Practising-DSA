Problem Statement: Given a number N, print the smallest and largest digits present in the number.

Examples:

Example 1:

Input: N = 2746

Output: 
Largest digit: 7
        
Smallest digit: 2
Explanation: By simply going through the digits of 
the number, we figure out the largest and smallest 
digit in the number.

Example 2:

Input: N = 23004

Output: 
Largest digit : 4
        
Smallest digit : 0

Explanation: By simply going through the digits of the number, we figure out the largest and smallest digit in the number.
